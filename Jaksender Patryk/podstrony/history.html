History of Joywave

Joywave is an American indie rock band from Rochester, New York, consisting of Daniel Armbruster, Joseph Morinelli, Benjamin Bailey, Paul Brenner, and Kevin Mahoney. The band was formed in 2010 and released their first EP, "88888," in 2011. Their debut album, "Koda Vista," was released in 2014, followed by "How Do You Feel Now?" in 2015 and "Content" in 2017. Joywave has gained a reputation for their unique blend of indie rock and electronic music, as well as their energetic live performances.
